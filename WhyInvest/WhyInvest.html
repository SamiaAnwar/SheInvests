Why Invest?

  • A better way to manage cash resources
  • Potential to earn more money in return
  • Many different ways to invest, such as stocks, bonds, mutual funds, various kinds of assets
  • Put savings into good use and have assets that can generate more money
  • Have a budgeting plan and set aside a certain amount of savings as an emergency fund as well as for investing
  • Automating investing so that a portion of your income goes directly into savings
